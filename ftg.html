What is the bare minunum require to start growing?

To start growing cannabis you only need 4 items.

  1. Seed(s)
  2. Water
  3. Paper Towl
  4. A cup

As crazy as it might seem, you only need these three items to start growing your own cannabis. Keep in mind without soil or water for you plant to take hold in; the plant will not survive. To see bare minunum required to keep your plant alive see the next section

How to start your Grow

To start a grow you need weed seeds. After you have obtained your cannabis seeds, you need to germinate the seeds. Germination can be done many ways. I will share the method that has best worked for me. If you find yourself struggling with the method I reccomend you look into other ways ther germinate your cannibis seeds.

How to germinate your weed seeds

  1. Place your seeds in a cup of water for 12-24 hours. ( I normally do around 24 hours)
  2. After the 24 hours dump some of the liquid and the seeds into a paper towel. (add enough water so the paper towel is saturated enough to be dripping)
  3. Check on the seeds every 12 hours or so. When you see a white root emerge from the seed its ready to be planted.
